Live

Plants are the silent architects of our world, weaving life into every corner of the Earth with patient grace. From the smallest blade of grass trembling in the morning dew to the towering trees that stretch toward the sky, they embody resilience, harmony, and quiet strength.


Wood

Wood is the quiet legacy of trees — a material shaped by time, patience, and the rhythm of seasons. Within every grain lies a story written in rings: years of sunlight, rain, wind, and growth carefully layered into a natural archive of life.
